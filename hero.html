Laptop Information

A laptop is a portable computer designed for mobility and convenience. It combines the essential components of a desktop computer into a compact and self-contained unit, making it suitable for use in various locations. Laptops are powered by rechargeable batteries, allowing users to work, browse the internet, and perform other tasks without being tethered to a power source.
